If your child does not like the program on Day 1, complete money will be refunded - no questions asked.

No. Beginner tracks are designed specifically for first-timers. We group kids by experience so they feel capable from Day 1.

Your child keeps the electronics (microcontrollers, motors, sensors) and projects they built so they can continue learning and iterating at home.
What if my child is advanced?
We have specific advanced tracks (Python Robotics, AI). Even within a group, challenges are open-ended allowing advanced students to push further.
